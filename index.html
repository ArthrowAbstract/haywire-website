About Us

The Haywire Club is a group of young tech enthusiasts from Mount Carmel School, Dwarka, New Delhi. This club was started back in the year of 2018 by a group of students who wished to bring about an electronic renaissance and help provide a healthy competing ground for budding electronic artists in our country. The mantle of this revolution of sorts has been passed down each year to new students and this legacy is taken and improved upon each year to bring the Ultimate tech showdown to those who hunger for proving their technological prowess. We are glad to announce that once again this year we will be hosting the new and improved Haywire 3.0 with multiple wide ranging events to allow anyone to show off their finesse with the only limiting factor being their skill. Hush tech enthusiasts! Chaos is in the air….. cause it's gonna get Haywire!
